JSP 2.0


"Jokhio Sketch Projects 2.0" is a redesigned and remastered version of the original Jokhio Sketchware projects app, brought back to life after the previous version stopped working. This updated app ensures a seamless experience with improved functionality and a modern interface, perfect for developers and enthusiasts who love creating with Sketchware.
